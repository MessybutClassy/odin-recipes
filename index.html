Pumpkin pie recipe

Description

What's better than an healthy and delicious pumpkin pie to end a perfect meal? With this easy to follow recipe, you'll learn how to prepare the most delicious Pumpkin pie. Let's dive right in the main ingredients:

  • 1 can pumpkin
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teasponn ground nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 unbaked pie crust
